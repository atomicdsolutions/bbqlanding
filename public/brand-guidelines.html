Purple Haze BBQ - Brand Guidelines

The official brand guide for Purple Haze BBQ. This document outlines all the essential elements of our brand identity.

About Our Brand

Purple Haze BBQ offers premium catering services in the DMV (DC, Maryland, Virginia) area, specializing in authentic barbeque for events of all sizes. Our brand represents quality, craftsmanship, and a memorable culinary experience that leaves a lasting impression.
